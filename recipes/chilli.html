Description:

easy 5-ingredient chili recipe and everyone we have made it for loved it. It is fast, simple, and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1(15 ounce) can kidney beans,drained
  • 1(15 ounce) can chilli beans, not drained
  • 2 tablespoons chilli powder, or to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

How to make Vegan Brownies

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
  2. Stir in kidney beans, chilli beans, and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil and stir in chilli powder. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 15 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
